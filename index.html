Our Heritage

Born from Decades of Craftsmanship

ENZO is the premium fabric brand born from the legacy of Humayun Ibrahim Textile — a name that has stood for quality and innovation in Pakistan's textile sector since 2003. Originally established in Kharadar, Karachi, the company began its journey manufacturing specialty yarns including Neps Slub, Dyed Yarn, and Mohair Blends.

Now proudly operating out of Lahore, ENZO extends this heritage into the world of refined fabrics — combining technical expertise with contemporary aesthetics for the modern discerning client.
